Much of the work I create is for university based projects or venues I have worked at. Occasionly I persue my interest in this field through personal side projects, enhancing my knowledge of the software. In one such venture, the sole power of my machine was not good enough to complete renders/animations within a feasible time frame.

Unlike some companies, I don't have an unlimited budget as I am a student, so I purchused a few (8) Intel OEM motherboards and networked the individual nodes to create a render farm. This greatly reduced render times at a reasonable price. Since then I have used the render farm to assist with other projects.
