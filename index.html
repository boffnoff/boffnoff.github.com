Guy with the best/biggest beard is the Alpha Dwarf
The guy with a respectable amount of facial hair is the Alpha Dwarf's right hand man
The guy who wears a pitiful display of stubble is suitable crushed and spat upon by all those lucky enough to have more hair than him.
